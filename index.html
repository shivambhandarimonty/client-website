Mehman Nahi, Parivar Hai

Kedarnath ki pavitra vadiyon mein sthit, Hotel Kedareshwaram aapko ek aisa anubhav deta hai jo shanti aur suvidha ka mishran hai. Humara hotel un yatriyon ke liye banaya gaya hai jo Baba Kedar ke darshan ke liye aate hain aur ek saaf, surakshit aur shuddh vatavaran ki talash mein hote hain.

Humara Satvik bhojan aur garam paani ki suvidha aapki thakan ko mita degi. Hum sirf kamre nahi dete, hum aapki yatra ko yaadgaar banane ki zimmedari lete hain.
